My Journey In Tech World!

Welcome, Everyone!

I am Pratik Raj, a passionate Full Stack Web Developer. After graduating in 2019, I began my career teaching at a coaching center while also providing home tuitions to students.

My tech journey started in 2021 when I noticed my friends diving deep into the IT sector. Being a curious learner, I explored various aspects of tech, but at that time, I found it quite complex and decided to move on.

Eventually, I landed as a System Engineer at Cognizant but was unfortunately terminated during training due to what was termed as low performance. Soon after, I secured a role as a Site Engineer in Haridwar, but my real desire was to become a Developer. I worked there for almost a year before deciding to pursue my passion.

A close friend suggested I join Masai School to train as a Full Stack Developer. In August 2023, I cleared the MSAT and began my journey in their intensive program starting October 4, 2023. Initially, the course was challenging as I was new to tech, but with persistence, I kept improving.

During my time at Masai, I developed strong skills in Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) and gained valuable experience through project-based learning. After completing the program, I received two job offers, one of which was from Masai School itself, where I am now employed as a Full Stack Developer.

The journey from being a student to becoming a developer at Masai School has been truly transformative. This is my story of growth and success in the tech world, and I hope you find it inspiring.
